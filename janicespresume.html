Introduction

I joined OTTA United in 2020 as the Regional Director of Health for the Student Response Initiative (SRI). Throughout OTTA's various transformations, our dedication to empowering youth has remained constant. This commitment led me to take on the role of President. I believe in the power of storytelling to empower youth and focus on improving access to public health services, especially for disadvantaged students. I'm excited to strengthen our infrastructure as OTTA enters a new chapter, ensuring we stay true to our mission amidst change.
